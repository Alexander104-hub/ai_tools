====== Stocknews AI ======

Stocknews AI is an artificial intelligence-based service that collects stock news from various sources. It provides features that can help users monitor market trends, identify potential investment opportunities, and make informed trading decisions.

With Stocknews AI, users can track the performance of stocks, sectors and the stock market as a whole. The service also offers the ability to set up individual alerts to receive important news about stocks of interest.

Stocknews AI's user interface is intuitive and provides quick and easy navigation, allowing users to quickly find the information they need.

**Stocknews AI Features:**

    • Receive personalized alerts about promotions of interest.
    • Monitoring of stocks, sectors and the market as a whole.
    • Fast and easy navigation with an intuitive user interface.
